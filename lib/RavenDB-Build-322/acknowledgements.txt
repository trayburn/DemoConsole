The Raven Datbase is making use of the following OSS projects:

* ManagedEsent project (http://codeplex.com/ManagedEsent)
* ICSharpCode.NRefactory from the SharpDevelop project (http://sharpdevelop.net)
* Newtonsoft.Json from James Newton-King (http://james.newtonking.com/pages/json-net.aspx)
* Mono Project - more specifically, parts of the Mono BCL implementations (http://mono-project.com)
* DotNetZip from http://dotnetzip.codeplex.com
